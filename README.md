## [üí° Image synthesis - illumination system *(2023)*](https://github.com/Mathmo0/Synthese-dimages)
<p>
From a C++ 3d engine base, I added image rendering to achieve different illumination of the scene.
</p>

### Ray tracer
<p align="center">
    <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Mathmo0/Synthese-dimages/main/images/RayTracer.png" >
</p>
<h5 align="center">left: 3D scene without rendering, right: image generated by ray tracing</h5>
<p>
A ray tracer that generates images with shadows, reflections and refractions (Fresnel) based on custom 3d models and textures.
</p>

### Photon mapping
<p align="center">
    <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Mathmo0/Synthese-dimages/main/images/photonMapping.png" >
</p>
<h5 align="center">left: 3D scene without rendering, middle: the scene after photon mapping, right: image generated by ray tracing (taking photons accumulation into account)</h5>
<p>
A photon mapping to generate photons that simulate the behavior of light as it interacts with different materials. For example, when generating caustics.
</p>

### Radiosity (Gauss-Seidel)
<p align="center">
    <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Mathmo0/Synthese-dimages/main/images/radiosity.png" >
</p>
<h5 align="center">top: 3D scene without rendering, bottom: 3D scene after using radiosity</h5>
<p>
Radiosity lighting calculation system using the Gauss-Seidel method. This simulates the physical behavior of light by calculating the quantities received and emitted on a surface.
</p>
